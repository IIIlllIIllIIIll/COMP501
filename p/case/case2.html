The Backdoor in the XZ Utils

Six months after the Linux incident, case 2 began. At first, "Jia Tan" submitted a patch to XZ Utils, and started to build trust in October 2021.

They continue to help XZ Utils until June 2023. In June 2023, they added the GNU indirect function (IFUNC) to the XZ Utils, which turned out to be one of the key steps for them to add the backdoor to XZ Utils. Also, to avoid detection, they disabled this feature on the OSS-Fuzz project.

In February 2024, they stopped hiding themself and submitted evil codes to the XZ Utils. Fortunately, their evil codes cause victims to take longer to log on to their servers, and this backdoor has been found before it causes mass destruction.
