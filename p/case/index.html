Cases

We identified two cases that could be the starting point of an emerging pattern, which has the potential to cause massive privacy leaks and financial losses.

The first case, or Case 1, provided a theory for attacks. The second case, or Case 2, is about someone conducting a real attack using a similar method compared to the first case.

Although we have no evidence on whether these two cases have any connection, we highly suspect that the attacker in the second case was inspired by the first case.
